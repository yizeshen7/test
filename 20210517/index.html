Why Not [Insert Popular Platform]?

Posted on 05/17/2021
One of the blog account that I follow on WeChat :D

When I started building this website, have asked myself this a few times as well, why not just post these on a WeChat offical account, a Facebook page, or even easier, stories on my Instagram :/. To be honest, I still don’t know, and I completely agree with how mature these social media are compare to mine, how many more people I may be able to reach if I have posted these there instead.

With that being said, do felt the flexibility/freedom was a major reason why I chose this. On this site I can post images, videos, short or long text, and even google map if I really feel like it. Realizing that I tend to change a lot as time progresses, having a site that can synchronusly adapted to who I am, felt this as a better fit for the purpose on recording my life in the coming years (if I have that much time that is :’) ).

Along with that, when I tried to sent posts on Facebook, Instagram and WeChat, always felt some kind of pressure, wanting to make the post I sent well polished. In contrast, here I simply do whatever I want, without thinking on who may be looking at the post, and how they would think towards it. Want this site to only be a place to record/express myself, the interactions that comes after it, was something that I try to stay away from. Without worrying about how others think towards the materials, do felt like I was more easily flush my mind out when writing. I won’t be surprised if you are able to find a way to reach me though, if you are reading this right now. I mean, at that point, well, I guess I will just go sit in my room and cry :( .

That being said though, I am not against the idea on bloggin in social medias. I followed a few blog-like acount on WeChat and Medium, and enjoyed them very much. Knowing that it’s more improtant on what you send, less on wher you sent it, I am still in this early stage of exploring this option, so wouldn’t be surprised if I change my mind later in the future. Nonetheless, formulating my ideas into words, record my life without worries, these are the goals I don’t wish to change, no matter which platform I ended up using :).
